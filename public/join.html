How do I Become a Member?

  • Register with ASCE National Chapter (Sign Up with your UCSD email)
  • Register with SCSE by filling out the SCSE Membership Form
  • Pay Student Chapter Fees (Freshmen/First-Year Transfers are free).
    Venmo @SCSEUCSD (enter “Member Dues” in description), or pay in person with cash, check, or credit card to the VP Finance.
    • Early Bird: $40 by November 16.
    • Normal: $45 after November 16.
    • ***FREE for freshmen and first-year transfers.
  • Attend Events
  • Join a Team
  • Volunteer at Outreach
  • Contact any Officer to get more involved
